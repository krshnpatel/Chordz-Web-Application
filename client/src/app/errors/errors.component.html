Possible Errors with ChordPro Formatting

  • Starting curly brace anywhere other than at the start of the line.
  • Ending curly brace anywhere other than at the end of the line (after removing whitespace).
  • Missing "title" directive before start of lyrics.
  • More than one "title" directive within the same "new_song" block.
  • Missing separator (":") or text for "title", "subtitle", "comment" or "define" directives.
  • Extra characters for "soc", "eoc", "sot" or "eot" directives.
